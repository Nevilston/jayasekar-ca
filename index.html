Regulatory Compliance Services

Proactive support to identify, manage, and control regulatory risks while reducing operational friction.

Our team of experienced compliance specialists provides high-quality and cost-effective services to help organizations manage regulatory risks. We assist you in identifying, managing, and controlling existing and future regulatory risks. Our people work at the strategic level to maximize competitive advantages from regulation, and at the operational level to minimize costs and disruptions, freeing up management time in the process. We believe in a proactive rather than a monitoring approach to regulation.

Financial Statement Audit

Highest assurance audits aligned with accounting standards for clarity, consistency, and transparency.

A financial statement audit provides the highest level of assurance of a company's financial statements. It is the process of official financial investigation to know the actual status of financial statements and ensure they are asserted as per the specific norms of international accounting standards. Our team of audit partners and managers is experienced with small, mid-size and large, family-owned businesses, growing companies, and start-up enterprises. Each audit engagement is customized to our client's needs to build trust and confidence through clarity, consistency, and transparency.

Internal Audit

Risk-based reviews to strengthen controls, improve processes, and reduce avoidable exposure.

We assist organizations in improving their internal function by measuring the effectiveness of existing processes and advising on the development of enhanced methodologies. Our internal audit team is not just a policing body but focuses on adding value to the business with effective controls at an optimum cost. Our risk-based internal audit plans focus on critical areas to enhance internal controls, improve processes, and help clients achieve wider assurance cost-effectively.

Performance & Proprietary Audit

Evaluation of plan execution, resource utilization, and adherence to authority and procedure.

Performance audit checks whether corporate plans are effectively executed and the reasons for variances in actual and planned performance. It also investigates if capital resources are properly utilized. Proprietary audit concerns executive decisions with an emphasis on public interest and financial discipline, to ensure actions are within the framework of sanction, authority, rule, procedure, and law. Our expert team undertakes proprietary audits focusing on correct implementation of plans, financial prudence, public interest, and prevention of wasteful expenditure.

Compliance Diagnostics

Accurate, tailored reporting and diagnostics to support confident business decisions.

Today's businesses have several compliance reporting requirements. With regard to accounting, compliance means ensuring a company's financial matters are handled in accordance with laws and regulations. At any point in time, a corporation should be able to provide accurate information about its accounts to its shareholders or to regulating authorities. We believe that businesses make better decisions when they have access to financial information that is accurate, suitably tailored, and delivered on a timely basis. K. Jayasekar & Co brings together experienced resources from relevant areas to address clients' tax and statutory accounting compliance and reporting requirements. Our compliance team focuses on ensuring accurate, high-quality financial reporting.

Risk Assurance Service

Structured risk management that improves productivity and protects organizational goals.

Risk management works through a framework that provides the organization an effective process and methods to follow to achieve desired results. Risk assessment helps identify potential problems with an organization's information, resources, and operations. Fine-tuning risk management systems assists in increasing productivity and achieving organizational goals, while helping the organization become familiar with operations, saving time, energy, and resources. The risk management and control systems are designed to ensure the business is focused on achieving its objectives, and risk assurance services facilitate identification of significant risks and mitigating them to the extent possible.
